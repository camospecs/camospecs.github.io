In 2025, I spent more time than any other year of my life on my arse and have thus amassed more fat than I would like. 

I don't typically like setting new year goals because I think I personally think I should be able to set and start them anytime, but this time it feels fine.

This page won't really have a form. I intend to keep it updated with progression and also a plan / guide for myself.


### Goal

Complete the Jurassic Coast Challenge (100k) in less than 20 hours. As a secondary goal, I'd also like get my weight to less than 80kg whilst maintaining or building muscle mass. 

![[Jurassic-Coast.jpg]]

However, to keep realistic, I will also have a plan B. That is to run the Race to the King, Chichester. 

To do this, I will:

- Run 4 - 5 days a week.
- Eat less than 2000 - 2100 calories on days other than my long run, where it will be 2700 - 3000 calories.
- Don't drink any alcohol.
- Don't consume nicotine.
- Sleep at least 8 hours per night.
